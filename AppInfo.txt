Speech To Text/Text To Speech App



Libraries used: System.Speech



How to use: Open with visual studio. The main form contains four elements: A textbox,two buttons and a slider


How it works:If you press the Speak button then the builtin voice synthesizer should speak the words you have typed. 
	     If you press the listen button and speak into the microphone or any default input audio device you have setup
	     then the words you have said should appear on the textbox. Finally If you Press the exit button it should exit
	     the application.
